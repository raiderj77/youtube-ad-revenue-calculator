How YouTube Ad Revenue Works

1

CPM (Cost Per Mille)

CPM means "cost per thousand impressions." Advertisers pay $X for every 1,000 ad views on your videos. Rates vary by niche and audience.

2

YouTube's 45% Cut

YouTube takes 45% of ad revenue. You keep 55%. So if advertisers pay $10 CPM, you earn $5.50 per 1,000 views.

3

Factors That Affect Earnings

Niche, audience location, video length, engagement, ad types, and seasonality all impact your actual revenue.
